Job Title: Business Analyst

Job Summary
We are seeking a highly analytical and detail-oriented Business Analyst to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for analyzing business processes, identifying improvement opportunities, and delivering data-driven recommendations that enhance operational efficiency, support strategic goals, and drive business growth.
Key Responsibilities
Collaborate with stakeholders to gather, document, and analyze business requirements.


Evaluate existing processes and systems to identify areas for optimization and automation.


Develop clear business cases, including cost-benefit analysis and ROI projections.


Create process maps, workflows, and functional specifications.


Translate business needs into technical requirements for development teams.


Support project implementation by conducting testing, training, and user acceptance validation.


Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure project outcomes and ongoing performance.


Prepare detailed reports, dashboards, and presentations for senior management.


Facilitate communication between business units, technical teams, and external partners.


Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Information Systems, or a related field.


3+ years of experience in business analysis, process improvement, or related roles.


Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.


Proficient in data analysis tools (e.g., Excel, SQL, Power BI, Tableau).


Familiarity with project management methodologies (e.g., Agile, Waterfall).


Excellent communication, presentation, and stakeholder management skills.


Ability to manage multiple projects with varying timelines and complexity.


Preferred Qualifications
Professional certifications such as CBAP, PMI-PBA, or Agile BA certification.


Experience with ERP, CRM, or workflow management systems.


Industry-specific knowledge in [Insert relevant industry, e.g., finance, healthcare, technology].

Job title: Compliance Risk Advisory Associate Consultant

About the job

We are looking for an individual interested in starting a career as a Risk Consultant within the Compliance team at Marsh Advisory. The opportunity is available at our Milan and Rome offices for a hybrid role.

Marsh Advisory is a management consulting firm that offers risk management services and solutions for large and medium-sized enterprises. Marsh Advisory is a subsidiary of Marsh, a leader in insurance brokerage, and is part of the international Marsh McLennan Group.

What awaits you:

You will support Senior colleagues in the following activities:

Implementation of Organizational and Management Models (Legislative Decree 231/01)
Implementation of Anti-Corruption Systems
Analysis of Corporate Governance in the ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) field
Data Protection compliance
Analysis and assessment of civil liability risks arising from defective products
Analysis of contractual risks
Support in Internal Audit activities
Requirements:

Master's degree in Economics
Advanced knowledge of the English language
Good knowledge of the Office package (particularly Excel and PowerPoint)
Proactivity, ambition, passion for the sector, problem-solving skills, and strong communication and interpersonal skills complete the profile
Theoretical knowledge of the aforementioned Compliance topics will be considered an added value
Why join the team:

You will have the opportunity to join through our Graduate Programme:

A personal and professional growth program that combines theoretical learning moments, thanks to the Italian and international training catalogue, with practical ones, through on-the-job training and continuous support from 3 figures: Tutor, Buddy, Mentor.
Learn Marsh's organizational structure thanks to an Induction Agenda where you will meet Senior Leaders from all Marsh teams.
Career progression with an initial 6-month internship aimed at permanent employment with an apprenticeship contract. For those with previous experience in the topics mentioned above, a direct contractual employment will be considered.

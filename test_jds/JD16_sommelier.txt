Wine Selection And Procurement

Conduct regular tastings to expand and update the restaurant's wine list.
Collaborate with distributors and wineries to source unique and high-quality wines.
Monitor inventory levels and manage the ordering process to ensure a well-stocked and diverse wine selection.

Customer Service

Provide personalized wine recommendations based on guests' preferences and menu choices.
Offer insights into the characteristics of various wines, vintages, and regions to enhance guests' understanding and enjoyment.
Ensure a high level of customer satisfaction by addressing inquiries, concerns, and special requests related to the wine program.

Wine Training And Staff Development

Conduct regular training sessions for restaurant staff to improve their wine knowledge and service skills.
Collaborate with the kitchen team to develop a deep understanding of the menu, enabling effective wine pairing recommendations.
Foster a culture of continuous learning and enthusiasm for wine among the restaurant staff.

Cellar Management

Maintain the wine cellar in optimal conditions, ensuring proper storage and organization.
Implement and oversee a rotating wine program to feature seasonal or special offerings.
Monitor and manage wine cellar inventory levels, conducting regular audits.

Collaboration With Culinary Team

Work closely with the chef and kitchen team to create wine pairings that complement the flavors and profiles of the menu items.
Attend menu tastings to develop a deep understanding of each dish, enabling informed wine selections.

Wine Events And Promotions

Plan and execute wine-related events, such as tastings, pairing dinners, and wine education sessions.
Collaborate with the marketing team to create promotions and marketing materials highlighting the wine program.

Adherence To Regulations

Ensure compliance with all local, state, and federal regulations related to the sale and service of alcoholic beverages.

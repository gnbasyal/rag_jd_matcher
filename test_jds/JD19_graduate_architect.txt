Job Description

Assist in creating architectural designs, drafting plans, and producing detailed drawings using software like AutoCAD or Revit.
Collaborate with architects and engineers on ongoing projects, contributing to design development, site visits, and documentation.
Research materials, construction techniques, and sustainable practices relevant to architectural projects.
Prepare visual presentations, 3D models, and renderings to communicate design concepts effectively.


We value collaboration and believe that in-person interactions are crucial for both our culture and client delivery.

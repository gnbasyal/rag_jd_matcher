Job title: Game Designer

Roles and Responsibilities-

Write clear and concise game design documents
Drive prototyping, mechanics, pacing, tuning, tutorial, usability, level ramp, story, meta-structure, aesthetics, and creative vision of projects assigned to you
Use a metrics-driven approach to game design, learning from users, as you drive retention, virality and monetization into the games design
Source ideas and draft proposals consisting of concept docs, development plans, product plans, and schedules.
Understand and contribute to overall company goals and sales/marketing requirements advising on portfolio direction and new game ideas and inventing and sourcing from third parties the best games to serve our target consumers.


Desired Candidate Profile-

Source ideas & draft proposals consisting of concept docs, development & product plans
Understand & contribute to overall company goals & marketing requirements on portfolio direction, and new game ideas.

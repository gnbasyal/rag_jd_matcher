Job title: AI Ethics Officer
Role Summary:
We are seeking a thoughtful and experienced AI Ethics Officer to lead the development and implementation of our AI ethics framework and governance practices. This pivotal hybrid role requires a deep understanding of AI technologies, ethical theories, relevant legal and regulatory landscapes, and policy development. You will work across technical and non-technical teams to embed ethical considerations into the entire AI lifecycle, from research and development to deployment and monitoring, ensuring our AI systems are fair, transparent, accountable, and beneficial.
Key Responsibilities:
Develop, implement, and maintain the company's AI ethics framework, principles, policies, and guidelines.
Conduct ethical risk assessments and impact analyses for AI projects and systems.
Provide expert guidance and consultation to product, engineering, research, and legal teams on AI ethics issues, including bias, fairness, transparency, accountability, privacy, and security.
Design and deliver AI ethics training and awareness programs for employees.
Establish and oversee processes for monitoring AI systems for ethical compliance and unintended consequences.
Collaborate with external stakeholders, including policymakers, academics, and civil society organizations, on AI ethics matters.
Stay abreast of emerging AI ethics research, best practices, regulatory developments, and societal concerns globally and within India.
Develop and manage mechanisms for reporting and addressing AI ethics-related concerns and incidents.
Advise senior leadership on AI ethics strategy and risk management.
Contribute to public discourse and thought leadership on responsible AI.
Ensure AI systems comply with relevant data privacy regulations (e.g., Digital Personal Data Protection Act in India, GDPR).
Required Qualifications:
Advanced degree (Master's or PhD) in a relevant field such as Ethics, Law, Public Policy, Computer Science (with a focus on AI/ML ethics), Philosophy, or Social Sciences.
5+ years of professional experience in a role focused on ethics, responsible technology, AI governance, or technology policy, with a significant portion dedicated to AI ethics.
Deep understanding of artificial intelligence, machine learning concepts, and their societal implications.
Strong knowledge of ethical theories, principles, and frameworks applicable to AI.
Familiarity with relevant legal and regulatory landscapes pertaining to AI, data privacy, and technology.
Proven ability to develop and implement policies and governance frameworks.
Excellent analytical, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.
Exceptional communication, interpersonal, and facilitation skills, with the ability to engage effectively with diverse stakeholders.
High degree of integrity and ethical judgment.
Preferred Qualifications:
Experience working directly with AI/ML development teams.
Published work or public speaking experience on AI ethics.
Experience in conducting ethical audits or impact assessments for AI systems.
Familiarity with international AI ethics guidelines and standards.
Legal background or qualification with a focus on technology law.
